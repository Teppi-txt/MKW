About Us

Hello, we are Minerva Knowledge!


We are a group of 4 first-year IBDP students and we aim to design various styles for notetaking templates, as well as some tips and study notes for IBDP students to use.


We have noticed there has been an increase in the usage of digital note-taking apps since many students love to use templates that will help them with their studies. Therefore, we wish to create templates that could support and be used by the IBDP community.


Why should you choose us?
  • Student-friendly!
  • Tons of different templates and tips for taking notes
  • Resources to help you focus and organise for a 45
